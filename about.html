Founded in Brussels in 1994, NOIR has spent three decades redefining what coffee can be. We don't just roast beans — we orchestrate an experience from volcanic soil to ceramic cup. Each batch is a meditation on time, terroir, and tradition.

Our Guiding Principles

Time. Terroir. Tradition.

Single-Origin

The Noir Chronicle

1994

Founder Alexandre Dumas opens the first NOIR atelier in Brussels' Grand Carmes district.

2003

Direct trade partnership established with 3 farms in Ethiopia and Panama.

2012

Introduction of 30-day oak barrel fermentation — a game-changing process.

2018

SQF Level 3 certification and launch of carbon-negative shipping.

2024

Opening of Tokyo Ginza and NYC SoHo tasting ateliers.
